this year did not go like i thought it would, and mostly in ways that i’m glad about. keep scrolling to see some highlights and hopes for the new year.

past things

somehow i’ve been doing this solo biz thing officially since july. what a whirlwind it’s been. i know at the beginning of the year i was way over my job and wanting something new, but still too scared to take this leap. i am still thrilled that i didn’t let fear keep me there, because i’ve been able to experience a whole new depth of life. i’ve stretched myself and i’ve had to trust myself, which isn’t always the easiest thing. so i guess it’s cool to know that my one little yes to me has led to a lot of great experiences. i’ve met some amazing people, worked with wonderful clients, traveled, and built a whole new life for myself that (even with the hard days) i’m really enjoying. it’s starting to feel like work is fitting into my life and not the other way around which i think is what leads to me thriving.

the idea for your goodlight i think has been in me for a while, but really came to be in march (march feels light years away.what is time, where does it go?). it’s crazy to think how the year would have gone if i just would have stayed put in my job. very glad i don’t have to find out though!!
